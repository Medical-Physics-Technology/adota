## Research ideas

#### 2025-04-22

- Implementation of the metric recognising types of materials through which protons travel. We need to assign a numerical values to different interfaces (e.g. bone-air should be quantified as a higher value than soft tissue-air). This will allow us to evaluate the impact of different materials on proton range and dose distribution.
- Construct a complex metric, perform analysis what formulation of the meta-metric (collected multiple different metrics through sime kind of equation or function) would be the most informative and useful for evaluating proton therapy plans. This could involve combining metrics such as dose homogeneity, conformity index, and range uncertainty into a single comprehensive metric that provides a more holistic assessment of treatment plans.
- Inspired by the paper "LARGE-SCALE LABEL QUALITY ASSESSMENT FOR MEDICAL SEGMENTATION VIA A VISION-LANGUAGE JUDGE AND SYNTHETIC DATA" we could explore the use of a model to predict gamma pass rate and range error based on the proton depth, the type of material the protons are passing through, and other relevant factors. This could help us to better understand the relationship between these variables and the overall quality of proton therapy plans, and potentially lead to improved treatment planning and delivery.
